I was born and raised in Istanbul, Turkey. After 22 years of consideration (I am 26, for reference) and obtaining my degree of triple-E from Bahcesehir Uni, I decided to move abroad in 2022. I have lived in France, Italy and Spain. I got my EMJMD in Medical Imaging Applications. As I fell in love with Barcelona during my thesis internship, I started my PhD at UPF’s one and only BCNMedTech. I am currently conducting research on reconstruction of 3D ultrasound prenatal faces (which I call 3DUPF for short, carelessly) under supervision of Prof. Gemma Piella and Federico Sukno.

To get to know me better, here is a list of things I like to do while I wait for my cluster to finish model training:

  • See a random old movie that no one’s heard of
  • Draw landscapes
  • Build in Cities: Skylines II
  • Dream about a solarpunk future
  • Learn languages
  • Watch long Youtube video essays about one or more of followings.
    • Mediterranean empires, mythology, Ireland, urban planning…
